Make it Able for Disable

Our Mission

Make it Able for Disable is a website that helps

businesses and people alike. 1 in 5 people in U.S.

have a disability according to the U.S. Census Bureau.

We are inspired by disable people and their courage and

want to make the world more able for the disable. Our website

is designed for the people to locate businesses that are acessiable

through surverys.
